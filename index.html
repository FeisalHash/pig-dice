PIG DICE

Welcome,below are the rules for pig dice

Have fun!!!

  • To play you'll need 2 players
  • One person rolls the dice until he/she either passes the turn or gets a 1
  • In the case of passing the turn all the 'turn' points accumulated get added to the Total scores
  • In the case of getting a 1 they lose all the 'turn' points accumulated and the other players turn comes immediately
  • First player to get 100 or more points wins
